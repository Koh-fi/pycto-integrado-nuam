We are nuam, the new Regional Holding that integrates the Santiago, Lima, and Colombia Stock Exchanges into a single market. Our mission is to develop our economies through a dynamic and updated offering that fosters business growth and delivers value to investors by offering a more attractive and diversified portfolio. We are confident that with nuam, we can create opportunities for prosperity for everyone.
